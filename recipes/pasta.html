Awesome pasta salad

Description

Pasta salad, known in Italian as insalata di pasta or pasta fredda, is a salad dish prepared with one or more types of pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked rotini pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing
  • 2 cucumbers, chopped

Steps

  1. Add pasta to boiling water
  2. Cook for 20mins
